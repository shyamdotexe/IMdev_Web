Thalapathy Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar is one of the most celebrated actors in Tamil cinema. Known for his charismatic screen presence, exceptional dance skills, and memorable performances, he has built a remarkable career spanning over three decades. Beyond cinema, he is admired for his philanthropic initiatives and strong connection with his fans.
